How Our Insurance Process Works

We make insurance claims stress-free with our streamlined process and expert guidance.

1

Initial Assessment

We inspect your vehicle damage and create a comprehensive estimate. Our certified technicians document everything needed for your insurance claim with detailed photos and measurements.

2

Insurance Contact

We contact your insurance company directly and provide all necessary documentation. Our team coordinates with your adjuster to schedule inspections and approve repairs.

3

Adjuster Meeting

We meet with your insurance adjuster on-site to review the damage and repair estimate. Our experience ensures all necessary repairs are approved and covered.

4

Deductible Payment

You pay only your deductible - we handle everything else. We bill your insurance company directly for all approved repairs, making the process seamless for you.

5

Professional Repairs

Our certified technicians complete all repairs to OEM standards using quality parts. We keep you updated throughout the repair process with photos and progress reports.

6

Final Delivery

Quality inspection and delivery of your restored vehicle. We provide all documentation for your records.
